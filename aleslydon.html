Welcome to Aleslydon

The Jewel of the Western Highlands

"Where all roads meet, and all knees bend."

Descend

Soot clings to the grand slate manors of Aleslydon, dark smudges against the city's glittering throat. In the high-tiered squares, spiced fat of roasted lamb pops over open braziers, and bright string melodies chase the sharp clink of merchants weighing diamonds.

♦

Yet a deep vibration thrums beneath the rolling hills, a constant grind that rattles the crystal in the noble estates—the city is always sharpening its teeth. While silk banners of the Aleslydon Songbird snap in the high winds, the city's true choir bleeds in the dark. Far below the streets, iron axles shriek against the stone, a jagged harmony to the wet, ragged gasps of the men hauling them.

♦

Spines bow, and blistered hands bleed to drag stolen crates of volatile Umbralith through the subterranean gloom. The air in these lightless forges tastes of ozone and scorched metal, this illicit haul feeding directly into sealed royal workshops where screaming gears and the heavy canvas-snap of prototype wings test their tethers in the dark.

Book One

The Call of Zeil'Galia

Available Now

Thrust into a world of shadow politics and ancient prophecies, Aden Roadson must flee the only home he's ever known. Hunted by demonic creatures and strange alien assassins, he must unravel the truth about his past—secrets tied to a mad king and a cosmic wound that bleeds sterile light across the sky.

With friendships fracturing under the weight of secrets and betrayals, Aden must decide who to trust before the forces hunting him tear the world apart.

The World

A Realm scarred by cosmic forces and ancient magic

The Bleeding Sky

Above the Realm hangs a wound—a jagged scar across the heavens that bleeds sterile light. No one alive remembers how it came to be, but its presence shapes everything: the politics, the magic, the wars, and the faith of every living soul beneath it.

The Realm is a place of harsh beauty and unforgiving danger, where workers' communes grind beneath the heel of noble houses, demonic forces press at the borders of reality, and forbidden magic whispers promises of power to those desperate enough to listen.

The Western Highlands

Chart your course: select a city to reveal its history.

Aleslydon

"Where all roads meet, and all knees bend."

Capital city of the West. Despite the beautiful pink and golden hues, the Songbird carries with it the cries of the lives it snuffed out in its unending quest for glory.

Urion

"Unbowed by Wind, Unbroken by Time."

A lonely but proud mountain city with tall walls and vast wealth disparity—but good food, and who could forget the wild mountain cats?

Grey-Haven

"Don't let the silence take you."

The ground here is cursed with a blight—it takes your emotions from you and makes you like a zombie. But it feels good to be numb.

Kreelavee

"By Steel and Scripture."

The city of disciples. Strict, god-fearing, and martial—faith and blade are forged as one in Kreelavee.

Varrhelm

"The Divine Threshold."

The city in the sky. They consider themselves in many ways above the fray—but are still very much involved in regional affairs. High magic and high technology converge here.

The horrors and wonders that stalk the Realm

Dragons

Apex Predator

Ancient and terrifying, the dragons of the Realm are not mere beasts. They are living engines of destruction, bound to forces older than the kingdoms they terrorize. Their fire is said to carry echoes of the world's creation.

Nightmarish beings from beyond the veil, the Hell'Shudiin are the demonic forces that hunt our heroes across the Realm. Their forms shift and writhe, and their presence warps the very air around them into something toxic and wrong.

Cadessi

Blood-Weavers

Known as the Blood-Weavers, the Cadessi practice a forbidden and revolting form of magic—manipulating the vital essence of living things. They are feared even among the Realm's most dangerous denizens, for their art twists life itself into a weapon.

The Author

✎

Bryce Charette

A lifelong enthusiast of storytelling and epic fantasy, Bryce Charette crafts character-driven stories set against grim, unforgiving landscapes. His works explore deep themes of life and death, the ego, and control.

His stories promise strong emotional character work and complex relationships—the kind that stay with you long after you've turned the last page.

He lives in Virginia, where he is currently working on the next saga in the Eternity's End series.
